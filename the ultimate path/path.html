About The Ultimate Path

I am born in a practicing muslim family.In my childhood, I loved my religion because my parents told me to love it, I practiced it for them. But in class 6, something led me to find a best friend who'll never misguide me.And guess what, I found my Allah, I found my ultimate path. so here, in this blog I will write about my journey to Islam, which areas of Islam amazes me the most and why I am in love with it..
